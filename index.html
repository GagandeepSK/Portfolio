Collins Aerospace - Shaft Storage Solutions

In our project, we were tasked with presenting innovative solutions for storing aircraft shafts of various sizes and materials. Balancing factors such as equipment mass, size, and portability, we conducted thorough research to develop a highly suitable containment system. The culmination of our efforts resulted in a compelling solution, which we enthusiastically presented to Collins Aerospace. This project was both exhilarating and technical, providing an opportunity to apply engineering skills and foster collaborative teamwork. (Click the Image)

Warwick Hyperloop - EDW Casing & Frame Design

Before the development of the actual Proof of Concept (POC), I played a key role in designing the motor mount and casing for the Electrodynamic Wheel (EDW). This pre-development phase was crucial for anticipating and addressing potential issues. The utilization of CAD software, specifically Fusion 360, proved invaluable, especially during motion studies. The CAD model closely resembled the test rig employed to measure the lift vector generated by magnetic fields. In this setup, the force was measured by a mass scale, providing a comprehensive visualization of the system. (Click the Image)
